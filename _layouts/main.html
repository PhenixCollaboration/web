PHENIX, the Pioneering High Energy Nuclear Interaction eXperiment, is the largest of the four experiments that have taken data at the Relativistic Heavy Ion Collider. Data-taking was finished in 2016 and the PHENIX Collaboration is now analyzing large data samples collected, prioritizing those with a unique physics reach.
